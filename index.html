Our Story

Designed in the City, Inspired by the Cosmos

NovaThread Collective is a fusion of modern architecture, cosmic wonder, and cultural rhythm. Founded by creatives who grew up in the pulse of the city, we build garments that adapt to the lives they live—dynamic, forward, and fearless.

We collaborate with global artisans to craft limited runs, ensuring every drop is as intentional as the people who wear it.

Est. 2020
