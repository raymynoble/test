Nightfall Layers with Aria K.

DJ and producer Aria K. invites us into her Brooklyn loft to style Drop V layers that move between rehearsal, rooftop, and stage lights.

Texture in motion

Aria pairs the Orbit Trench Coat with the Flux Knit Crew, letting soft knit texture peek out from beneath bonded cotton. “It feels like armor with a heartbeat,” she says, cinching the belt before stepping onto her balcony.

A tonal Cascade Wool Cap keeps the look grounded. “When I’m crate digging or biking between sets, I need pieces that breathe but still look intentional,” Aria explains.

From studio to stage

For late sets, Aria swaps into the Lumen Track Set. The reflective piping catches the club strobes while the moisture-wicking interior keeps the tempo comfortable. “I can pack the whole set into my backpack after sunrise,” she laughs.

She layers the Axis Modular Vest over the track jacket, removing the cargo pods to hold USB sticks and headphones. “Hands-free gear that still looks sharp? That’s the dream.”
