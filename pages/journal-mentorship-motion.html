Mentorship in Motion

How our youth arts program is fueling next-gen designers with resources, scholarships, and industry access.

Studio sessions

Every quarter, NovaThread opens the studio for emerging designers to prototype garments using our deadstock fabrics. Mentors from the production team guide them through pattern-making, materials sourcing, and sustainable scaling.

Participants receive stipends and access to our Horizon Lounge Hoodie and Meridian Pleat Trouser patterns to remix for their collections.

Scholarship impact

  • 12 designers funded in the past year to study textile innovation at partner universities.
  • Over 4,000 yards of fabric diverted from landfills through our mentorship labs.
  • New capsule collaborations launching every season with alumni designers.

Get involved

Want to mentor, donate materials, or nominate a creative? Email community@novathread.co and we’ll connect you with our program coordinator. Together we’re building a fashion future rooted in equity and imagination.
